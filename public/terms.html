Managed Wallets: If you opt for a managed wallet, we generate and hold the private keys on your behalf. 4. Fees

We charge a percentage-based transaction fee on confirmed payments. Fee rates are volume-based and calculated on a rolling 30-day basis:

  • $50,000 - $250,000/month: 0.5%
  • $250,000+/month: 0.3%

Fees accumulate and are invoiced per billing cycle (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly depending on volume tier). You receive 100% of each payment immediately — fees are billed separately. Failure to pay invoiced fees may result in account suspension.
