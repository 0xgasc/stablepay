FAQ

How do progressive brackets work?

Like income tax — each bracket applies only to volume in that range. Your first $10k always pays 1%, regardless of total volume. Only amounts above each threshold get the lower rate.

When do I get PRO features for free?

When your 30-day rolling volume reaches $5,000, PRO features unlock automatically — no payment needed. Or pay $19/mo in crypto to unlock immediately.

How do I pay fees?

Fees accumulate monthly. You pay them in stablecoins (USDC/USDT) from your dashboard. You receive 100% of every payment upfront — fees are invoiced separately.

What chains and tokens do you support?

Solana, Ethereum, TRON, BNB Chain, Base, Polygon, and Arbitrum. USDC, USDT, and EURC on all chains where available.
