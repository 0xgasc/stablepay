Frequently Asked Questions

How does billing work?

For STARTER and PRO plans, you pay the greater of: monthly subscription fee OR transaction fees. For FREE and ENTERPRISE, you only pay transaction fees.

Can I upgrade anytime?

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately and billing is prorated.

What happens if I exceed my volume limit?

We'll notify you when you reach 75% and 90% of your limit. Once exceeded, you'll need to upgrade to continue processing payments.

Which blockchains do you support?

Base, Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, Solana (mainnet + testnet). FREE tier gives you 1 chain pair (e.g., BASE testnet + mainnet). Paid plans unlock all 5 chains (10 networks total).
