FAQ

How does billing work?

We deduct our fee from each payment before it reaches your wallet. You receive (payment amount - fee) directly. No invoices, no surprises.

How do volume tiers work?

We calculate your 30-day rolling volume. When you cross a tier threshold, your fee rate automatically decreases for all new transactions.

What's included in Test Mode?

Unlimited testnet transactions to build your integration. Plus 10 real mainnet transactions and $100 volume to verify everything works before going live.

Which blockchains do you support?

Base, Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, and Solana. Each with mainnet and testnet. All chains available on all tiers.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. The transaction fee is all you pay. No setup fees, no monthly minimums, no withdrawal fees. Gas fees are paid by the customer.

How do I go live from Test Mode?

Just switch to mainnet in your dashboard. No approval needed. Start accepting real payments immediately.
